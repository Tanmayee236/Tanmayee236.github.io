ABOUT ME

I am a PhD student in Astrophysical Sciences and Technology. I work on mathematical modelling of neutron stars and use data analysis techniques to validate the results. When I am not merging neutron stars, I work on understanding how to use data and machine learning techniques for explaining everyday phenomenon.
